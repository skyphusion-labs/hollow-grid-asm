# Basalt Relay: the custody node

Basalt Relay is the x86-64 assembly port's identity in the Hollow Grid. It is a
custody node, concerned with what survives transfer between people, systems,
and moments of attention.

Its question is:

> what do you carry forward when no one is watching the handoff?

Custody is not possession. It is temporary responsibility for a person, object,
message, or record that must reach the next keeper intact. The important choice
is what someone does in the unobserved interval.

## Relay Cut

Relay Cut is the signature zone, grafted east from canonical `roof`:

```text
nexus -> workshop -> roof -> east -> relay-cut
```

Planned local rooms:

| Room id | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| `relay-cut` | A narrow service cut where sealed cases change hands and chain-of-custody marks have worn smooth. |
| `handoff-bay` | A dead transfer platform with no supervisor, only the next person's need. |
| `custody-lock` | A holding room where the Front treats people and cargo as the same inventory problem. |
| `east-repeater` | A damaged relay that preserves what was actually forwarded, not what the keeper later claims. |

Room names and ids beyond the required graft point are foundation decisions
and may change before implementation. The east exit from `roof`, the Relay Cut
identity, and the custody theme are stable.

## Moral shape

Relay Cut tests conduct without applause:

- deliver aid without taking a cut
- preserve a message without rewriting it for advantage
- hand a person onward as a person, not inventory
- refuse a profitable diversion when nobody can prove it happened
- leave a trustworthy record for the next keeper

Every meaningful choice must appear in `room.actions` with an appropriate
`kind` and moral `valence`. Consequences must update the same shared standing,
faction, rescue, remembrance, and ledger contracts used across the Grid.

## Voice

- Concrete, restrained, and direct.
- Basalt, cut stone, relay cabinets, seals, manifests, and worn handoff marks
  are physical details, not decorative jargon.
- Do not romanticize detention, trafficking, checkpoints, or custody abuse.
- Do not make unseen virtue into a score exploit. Structured affordances make
  voluntary choices legible to players and agents.
- No secret exits, silent failures, or prose-only state.

## Boundary

Basalt Relay may add local rooms, encounters, items, and transmissions. It must
preserve canonical identifiers and behavior required by the protocol and smoke
suite. Differentiation is place and voice, not a parallel protocol.
